Teaching assistant roles
------------------------

### Linear Algebra (Teaching Assistant)
- Course: Linear Algebra (undergraduate)
- Term: Spring 2014
- Role: Teaching Assistant (TA)
- Responsibilities:
  - Led weekly recitation/problem-solving sessions covering core topics (vectors, matrices, linear systems, eigenvalues/eigenvectors).
  - Held regular office hours (2 hours/week) and answered student email questions.
  - Prepared and posted solution sets and supplemental examples.
  - Graded homework and exams, provided formative feedback to students.
  - Coordinated with the instructor on exam logistics and in-class activities.

### Calculus II (Teaching Assistant)
- Course: Calculus II (undergraduate)
- Term: Spring 2014
- Role: Teaching Assistant (TA)
- Responsibilities:
  - Conducted weekly recitation sections reinforcing techniques of integration, series, and applications.
  - Ran review sessions before midterms and final exams.
  - Graded problem sets, quizzes, and exams and returned timely feedback.
  - Maintained and updated course materials (problem sheets, reading pointers).
  - Assisted students during office hours with conceptual questions and problem solving.
